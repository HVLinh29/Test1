Lumi’s new Premium Home Controller is a smart home hub that offers more power and flexibility for users. It has improved hardware and features that can support complex smart home scenarios. The Premium Home Controller is the ultimate solution for smart home enthusiasts.

1. Hardware

  • The square shape and the black glossy cover give it a sleek and modern look.
  • The cover is made of premium ABS plastic that is strong and resistant to impact, ensuring durability and quality.
  • The product’s basic color is matte gray silver, which matches the cover and adds to the design’s sophistication.
    The wall-mountable foot adjusts in two directions, providing installation flexibility and customisation.

2. Features

2.1. Quad-core processor for multi-tasking and super-fast processing:

With a Quad-core ARM® Cortex®-A9@1.2GHz processor, the device can handle multiple tasks quickly and efficiently. It can also support up to 450 devices and has an optimal processing time of 150ms, allowing you to connect and manage all your smart home devices via smartphone or voice commands. You can also create over 200 rules, scenes, and schedules to customize your living scenarios according to your preferences.

The device has 512MB RAM and 4GB Flash memory for data storage and stable operation. It also has two 2.4G/5.8G WiFi receivers for the best smart home control experience. It also supports the Zigbee and BLE Mesh communication standards, making it easy to connect to a variety of electrical equipment.

2.2. Maintaining stable control even when there is no internet thanks to the built-in LTE-4G feature

The Premium Central Controller offers LTE-4G connectivity, which enables customers to control their smart home appliances from their smartphone even without internet access. Moreover, the LTE-4G feature protects the device from viruses and hackers that may come from unknown 4G DCOM sources. Furthermore, the central controller’s local network ensures that the device settings are always updated and consistent.

2.3. Multi-modal control and automated living scenarios

You can use your voice to control all of your electrical appliances at home through the Lumi Life+ app on your smartphone or smart speaker, from any place at any time. Moreover, you can set up smart living scenarios to automate your house according to your needs and preferences. You can customize your home scenarios with more than 200 rules, scenes, and routines, and you can even trigger them with a single touch on your smartphone. The Premium Central Controller also helps you save energy by allowing you to remotely monitor and adjust your devices, and avoid unnecessary waste.

2.4. Complete security with an external SIMCOM device

Customers can use HC to combine a variety of smart camera and sensor products, creating a secure solution for their family. HC Premium supports Simcom, a 4G mobile network device that uses a SIM card to connect to LTE-4G network even when Wifi/LAN network fails. This ensures that the homeowners can receive alerts via SMS, phone calls and Lumi Life+ app when there is an unauthorized intrusion. This function is highly useful in security scenarios since it allows homeowners to stay up to date on their home security even when they do not have access to the internet. Furthermore, Lumi encrypts and securely stores data on the cloud. The data server is located in Vietnam and upgraded yearly to guarantee absolute security.
